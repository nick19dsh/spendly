Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as pages visited and actions taken, used to diagnose errors and improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To provide and maintain your account and expense records.
  • To display summaries, charts, and reports within the app.
  • To send essential account emails (e.g., password resets).
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard measures — including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords — to protect your information from unauthorised access.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces. Google may log font requests; see Google's privacy policy for details.

We do not share your personal or financial data with any third party for their own purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app.
